About Us

Made in Pune.
Built with Passion.

Crafteon Labs was born out of a simple idea — that anyone should be able to hold their imagination in their hands. We're a small, student-founded 3D printing studio based in Pune, driven by a love for making things that matter.

What started as a personal project with a single printer has grown into a creative studio serving customers across Maharashtra and beyond. Every product we make is designed, printed, and finished in-house — no outsourcing, no compromises.

Whether it's a birthday gift, a collector's figurine, or a functional engineering prototype, we put the same level of care into every order. We believe great products come from attention to detail and genuine enthusiasm for the craft.
